Ride Sharing Documentation

Introduction

This documentation provides information on how to use the ride-sharing feature in our application.

How to Request a Ride

  1. Open the ride-sharing app.
  2. Select your pickup location.
  3. Choose your destination.
  4. Select your ride type (e.g., car, bike, etc.).
  5. Confirm your ride request.

How to Become a Driver

Follow these steps to register as a driver:

  • Sign up on the driver portal.
  • Complete the required documentation.
  • Attend the driver onboarding session.
  • Start accepting ride requests.

FAQs

Q: How do I cancel a ride?
A: You can cancel a ride from the app by going to your ride history and selecting the cancel option.

Q: How are fares calculated?
A: Fares are calculated based on distance, time, and ride type.
